Services & treatments

Hands-on therapy, backed by a real plan

Every patient gets a full assessment first — then a treatment mix chosen for your goals, not a one-size-fits-all routine.

Manual therapy

Skilled hands-on mobilisation and soft-tissue work to ease pain and restore movement.

Therapeutic exercise

Personalised strengthening and mobility programmes you can progress safely week by week.

Electrotherapy

TENS, ultrasound and other modalities to calm pain and support tissue healing.

Gait & balance training

Retraining walking, balance and coordination using parallel bars and guided practice.

Geriatric care

Gentle, dignified physiotherapy to keep older adults mobile, safe and independent.

Wellness & posture

Workplace ergonomics, posture correction and prevention so the pain doesn’t come back.

Your rehab pathway

A clear path from pain to recovery

No guesswork. Here’s exactly what happens from the moment you reach out.

Reach out

Send a WhatsApp message or fill the booking form. Tell us what’s wrong — we reply fast and help you pick a time.

Full assessment

A physiotherapist examines you properly, explains what’s going on in plain language, and sets clear goals with you.

Your treatment plan

You get a tailored plan — sessions, home exercises and a realistic timeline. Clinic visits or home care, your choice.

Recover & stay well

We track your progress, adjust as you improve, and give you the tools to keep the pain from returning.
